Hello, I'm Benen


I'm an always-learning builder and creator


In a past life, I was extremely interested in finance and investing. I wrote a series of articles on a popular investment website and regularly achieved over 10,000 readers. I even worked for a "tiger cub" hedge fund in an investment role. I've examined funeral homes in Canada, furniture makers in Australia, steel makers in India and everything in between.


Then I realized it was all meaningless


Since then, I've been exploring the startup world. I'm heavily involved in the entrepreneurship scene on campus and am currently working on a product that's beginning the go-to-market process.


Independence is the most important thing in my life. That why even though I'll be working as a Product Manager for a healthcare data startup this summer, I hope I'll be doing well enough that I'll quit my job and never have to have one ever again.
